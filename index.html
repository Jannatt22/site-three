About Us

Riverstone Library was redesigned to inspire literacy, creativity, equal opportunity and lifelong learning and will play a key role in the urban community. At this library, we foster knowledge and a passion for learning. Riverstone Library first opened its doors to the public on September 23, 1991. After serving the community for 30 years, the library closed on March 20, 2021, to undergo a major renovation to meet the growing and changing needs of its customers and business priorities. The library reopened in late 2023.

Riverstone Library is more than just a place to borrow books—it's a vibrant community hub where people of all ages come to learn, connect, and discover new possibilities. With an extensive collection of resources, ranging from physical books to digital media, the library nurtures a passion for reading and supports lifelong learning. It also hosts a variety of programs and events designed to engage diverse interests, making Riverstone Library an essential space for personal growth, creativity, and social connection.
